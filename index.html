Welcome to Caffeine & Co, where every cup tells a story. Our cozy café is more than just a place to grab your favorite brew; it's an experience that awakens your senses and warms your soul.
As you step inside, the aroma of freshly ground coffee beans envelops you, inviting you to linger a little longer. Our baristas, true coffee artisans, craft each cup with care, ensuring that every sip is a moment of bliss. We're all you need rolled into one. We are your pit stop to and from the office, the place you treat your friends and the space where you take time to relax. Dine In and Take Out

Order Deadlines/Restrictions

•All orders must be paid in full at time of order.
• All orders require 48 hours to prep. Overly large orders may require more time, but we will reach out to you in that case.
• Orders can be picked up during our operating hours, 7 days a week.


Packaging

• We provide food packaging that can be either recycled or composted.
• Service-ware is not automatically supplied, if you require it, please select the option when ordering.
• Reusable carafes must be returned within 48 hours to avoid late charges ($50 per day).
• Catering orders will be packaged on communal trays and boxes. If you require individual packaging please select that option when ordering.


Dietary Restrictions

• Please note that this is not a gluten-free environment.
• While we make every effort to accommodate dietary restrictions and eliminate the possibility of cross contamination, it is not a guarantee.
• Should you require special dietary accommodations, please let us know in the comments.


Delivery

• Please note we do not offer delivery at this time.
• If delivery is a must, please reach out to us about arranging something, additional fees may be required.
